# Story Backlog

This file tracks candidate epics and unsliced or partially sliced work.

Use it when work is accepted enough to deserve durable grouping, but not yet
small enough to express entirely through one story packet.

## Backlog Fields

| Epic ID | Working Name | Status | Source Intake | Product Docs Touched | Risk Level | Next Slicing Action | Blocked By / Decision Needed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Add rows only when accepted intake or accepted product truth creates a real epic candidate.

## Status Guidance

- `unsliced`: epic exists, but story decomposition is not yet ready
- `sliced`: at least one real story candidate exists
- `in_progress`: active stories are being executed from this epic
- `blocked`: next slice depends on unresolved truth or decision
- `retired`: grouping is no longer relevant

## Backlog Rules

- backlog rows should come from accepted intake or accepted product truth
- backlog rows should not be speculative parking lots
- if grouping adds no clarity, prefer direct story creation instead
